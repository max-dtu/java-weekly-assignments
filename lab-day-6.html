Module 13, Part 1: Lab Day — Exam Simulation

Module 13, Part 1: Lab Day — Exam Simulation#

This first part of this Module does not introduce new contents: instead, the teacher will make available a sample exam paper DTU Learn. The sample exam paper is part of the assessments for week 13 — and more assignments are provided in part 2 of this Module.

To obtain the sample exam paper for this Lab Day:

  1. open the course page on DTU Learn (https://learn.inside.dtu.dk/d2l/home/215298);

  2. navigate to Contents → Sample Exam Papers;

  3. select the Sample Exam 02 PDF file.

Important

The exam paper PDF will become downloadable at the beginning of this Lab Day.

The recommendation is: try downloading and solving the sample exam paper during the lab, doing much as you can within the 4-hour time limit, as you would do in the final exam.

Besides, the deadline for submitting the weekly assessments on DTU Autolab will be one week afterwards, as usual.
